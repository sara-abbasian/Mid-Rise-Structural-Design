# Mid-Rise Structural Design

A complete structural analysis and design project involving two seven-storey buildings: one reinforced-concrete structure and one steel-frame structure with a composite roof system.

## Project Overview

The project included structural-system selection, gravity and lateral load calculations, ETABS modelling, member design, foundation design in SAFE, and preparation of architectural and structural drawings in AutoCAD.

Both buildings were designed in accordance with applicable Iranian structural codes and standards.

## Project Scope

### Reinforced-Concrete Building

- Reinforced-concrete beams, columns, and slabs
- Gravity and lateral load analysis
- Member sizing and reinforcement design
- Structural stability assessment
- Foundation design

### Steel Building

- Steel beams, columns, and bracing
- Composite roof system
- Gravity and lateral load analysis
- Member sizing and verification
- Foundation design
